35 Years of Academic Distinction

For over three decades, Pakistan Ideal Public High School (PIPS) has stood as a beacon of educational excellence and moral leadership at Chowk Philloura. Founded on timeless values, our institution has consistently delivered phenomenal academic milestones, shaping generations of highly capable, disciplined, and visionary individuals.

Our exceptional track record of performance is driven by a prestigious, highly experienced faculty dedicated to nurturing intellectual curiosity. We do not just teach; we empower minds, cultivate outstanding character, and maintain a competitive educational standard that consistently makes our community proud.

Our Academic Structure

We provide a comprehensive educational pathway tailored to cultivate strong foundations and advanced logical skills across all age groups:

Junior Wing (Playgroup to Primary)

Focusing on early cognitive skills, creative learning environments, fundamental literacy, and basic behavioral development.

Senior Wing (Middle to Matric)

Rigorous preparatory curriculum specializing in science and arts frameworks to excel in board examinations.

Official Board Affiliation

Pakistan Ideal Public High School is fully registered and proudly affiliated with the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (BISE) Gujranwala, ensuring our matriculation certificates follow standard, elite national metrics.
